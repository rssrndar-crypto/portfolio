Comprehensive Maintenance Tool Inventory

The platform tracks and manages a complete tooling ecosystem across multiple maintenance categories:

1. General Hand Tools

Common tools used across airframe, engine, and avionics maintenance.

  • Torque Wrench – Applies precise torque to bolts/nuts
  • Socket Set & Ratchets – Fastener removal/installation
  • Combination Wrench Set – For nuts and bolts in various sizes
  • Screwdrivers (Flat/Phillips/Torx) – General fastening/removal
  • Allen (Hex) Key Set – Used for hex-head bolts
  • Safety Wire Twisting Pliers – Secures fasteners with locking wire
  • Rivet Gun & Bucking Bar – For rivet installation/removal
  • Hammer & Mallet Set – For mechanical adjustments
  • File Set – Surface finishing
  • Measuring Tape / Scale – Length measurement

2. Precision & Measuring Instruments

Used for inspection, alignment, and calibration.

  • Vernier Caliper – Linear measurement up to 0.02 mm accuracy
  • Micrometer (Inside/Outside) – Fine measurement of diameter/thickness
  • Dial Indicator – Alignment, run-out, concentricity checks
  • Feeler Gauge – Gap/clearance measurement
  • Protractor / Angle Gauge – Checks deflection angles
  • Depth Gauge – Bore and depth measurement
  • Digital Multimeter (DMM) – Voltage, current, resistance checks
  • Clamp Meter – Non-contact current measurement
  • Megger (Insulation Tester) – Checks insulation resistance
  • Oscilloscope – Analyzes electronic signals
  • Pressure/Vacuum Gauge – System pressure tests

3. Aircraft-Specific Tooling (Airframe)

Used for maintenance of control surfaces, landing gear, fuselage, etc.

  • Jack (Tripod / Axle / Wing) – Lifts aircraft for gear maintenance
  • Control Surface Rigging Tools – Adjusts control linkages
  • Cable Tension Meter – Measures flight control cable tension
  • Drill Motor & Drill Bits – For drilling and rivet removal
  • Rivet Cutter / Rivet Squeezer – For rivet installation
  • Sheet Metal Brake & Shear – For forming sheet metal
  • De-burring Tool – Removes sharp edges post-drilling
  • Composite Repair Kit – Resin, vacuum bags, curing tools
  • Paint Thickness Gauge – Checks coating thickness

4. Engine & Powerplant Tools

Used for piston/turbine engine maintenance and inspection.

  • Borescope / Videoscope – Visual inspection of engine internals
  • Compression Tester – Cylinder compression check
  • Fuel Pressure Tester – Measures fuel line pressure
  • Torque Adapter – Ensures accurate torque on difficult access areas
  • Spark Plug Socket & Gap Gauge – For piston engines
  • Propeller Balancing Tool – Static/dynamic balancing
  • Oil Filter Cutter – Inspects debris in oil filters
  • Fuel Drain Kit – For fuel sampling and contamination checks
  • Lockwire Pliers – Safety wiring on engine fasteners
  • Magneto Timing Light – Sets ignition timing

5. Avionics & Electrical Tools

Used for navigation, communication, and control systems.

  • Avionics Test Set (NAV/COMM) – Tests VHF, HF, transponder, etc.
  • Pitot-Static Tester – Simulates airspeed and altitude pressure
  • Altimeter Test Set – Verifies pressure altitude systems
  • Transponder Tester – Checks Mode A/C/S responses
  • GPS Simulator – For GNSS system checks
  • Circuit Breaker Tester – Checks CB operation
  • Wire Stripper & Crimper Set – Wiring assembly/repair
  • Continuity Tester – Electrical continuity check
  • Cable Harness Board – For wiring loom fabrication
  • Connector Pin Extractor – Removes electrical connector pins

6. NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) Equipment

Used for structural integrity checks.

  • Ultrasonic Flaw Detector – Detects cracks internally
  • Eddy Current Tester – Surface/subsurface crack detection
  • Magnetic Particle Inspection Kit – Detects surface defects on ferrous materials
  • Dye Penetrant Kit – Crack detection on non-ferrous materials
  • Radiography (X-ray) Equipment – Internal structural analysis
  • Thermographic Camera – Detects delamination/heat variations

7. Ground Support & Safety Equipment

Essential for maintenance environment safety and aircraft handling.

  • Tow Bar / Tug – Aircraft towing
  • Wheel Chocks – Prevent rolling
  • Safety Cones / Barriers – Work area safety
  • Fire Extinguishers – Mandatory for maintenance bays
  • Grounding Cable – Prevents static discharge
  • Hydraulic Test Stand – For system pressure testing
  • Nitrogen/Oxygen Service Cart – Tire and oxygen servicing
  • Engine Hoist / Sling – For engine removal
  • Battery Charger / Tester – For aircraft batteries
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) – Helmets, gloves, goggles

8. Calibration & Test Instruments

Critical for maintaining tool accuracy and regulatory compliance.

  • Torque Wrench Calibrator – Verifies torque accuracy
  • Pressure Calibration Pump – Calibrates pressure gauges
  • Temperature Calibrator – Verifies thermocouples/sensors
  • Multimeter Calibrator – Calibrates electrical instruments
  • Dimensional Calibration Block Set – For micrometers/calipers
  • Scale & Weight Sets – For load cell verification
  • Software License Keys – For avionics calibration systems

9. Consumables & Support Items

To be tracked in inventory.

  • Sealants / Adhesives – For structural repairs
  • Lubricants / Grease – For bearings and joints
  • Safety Wire – For fastener locking
  • Cleaning Solvents – For general cleaning
  • Paints / Primers – Surface coating
  • Shop Towels / Rags – Cleaning

10. Suggested Digital Data Fields for Each Tool

The platform supports the following data structure for comprehensive tool tracking:

  • tool_id – TOOL-0001 (Unique ID)
  • tool_name – Torque Wrench (Tool identification)
  • category – Measuring Tool (Classification)
  • serial_number – TW-45678 (Tracking)
  • manufacturer – Snap-On (OEM detail)
  • calibration_due – 2025-12-15 (Calibration tracking)
  • assigned_to – EMP-102 (Engineer ID)
  • location – Hangar A / Toolbox 3 (Storage)
  • status – Available / In Use / Under Calibration (Tool status)
  • condition – Good / Damaged / Serviceable (Readiness)
  • last_used – 2025-10-28 (Usage record)
  • remarks – Requires calibration soon (Maintenance note)
