Tool Management System

The platform tracks 10 categories of maintenance tools:

  • Hand Tools – Wrenches, screwdrivers, pliers, rivet guns
  • Measuring Instruments – Calipers, gauges, multimeters, testers
  • Airframe Tools – Jacks, drills, sheet metal equipment
  • Engine Tools – Borescopes, compression/fuel pressure testers
  • Avionics & Electrical – Test sets, wire crimpers, continuity testers
  • NDT Equipment – Flaw detectors, thermal cameras
  • Safety Equipment – Chocks, extinguishers, hoists, PPE
  • Calibration Tools – Calibrators for torque, pressure, temperature
  • Consumables – Sealants, lubricants, safety wire, cleaners

Track Each Tool With

  • Tool ID & name
  • Category & serial number
  • Calibration due date
  • Assigned to engineer
  • Current location & status
  • Condition (Good/Damaged/Serviceable)
  • Last used date & maintenance notes
